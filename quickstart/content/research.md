### Research

Broadly speaking, I am quite interested in **discrete mathematics**, especially number theory, combinatorics, and finite fields. For my PhD, I am currently working on various problem related to equations of restricted height. During my bachelor and master studies, I did a total of two final projects on some objects over finite fields. However, I am open for learning other topics.

### Publications
- **Muhammad Afifurrahman**, Alina Ostafe, and Igor E. Shparlinski. *Statistics of ranks, determinants and characteristic polynomials of rational matrices*. [Preprint](https://arxiv.org/abs/2401.10086).
- **Muhammad Afifurrahman**. *Some counting questions for matrix products*, accepted to Bulletin of Australian Mathematical Society. [Preprint](https://arxiv.org/abs/2306.04885).
- **Muhammad Afifurrahman** and Aleams Barra. *Determining Sidon polynomials in Sidon sets over $\mathbb{F}_q \times \mathbb{F}_q$*, accepted to Journal of Algebra Combinatorics Discrete Structures and Applications. [Preprint](https://arxiv.org/abs/2111.08886).

AIP Conference Proceedings 2423, 060002 (2021). [Preprint](https://arxiv.org/abs/2111.08352).
