Research

Broadly speaking, I am quite interested in discrete mathematics, especially number theory, combinatorics, and finite fields. For my PhD, I am currently working on various problem related to equations of restricted height. During my bachelor and master studies, I did a total of two final projects on some objects over finite fields. However, I am open for learning other topics.

Publications

  • Muhammad Afifurrahman, Alina Ostafe, and Igor E. Shparlinski. Statistics of ranks, determinants and characteristic polynomials of rational matrices. Preprint.
  • Muhammad Afifurrahman. Some counting questions for matrix products, accepted to Bulletin of Australian Mathematical Society. Preprint.
  • Muhammad Afifurrahman and Aleams Barra. Determining Sidon polynomials in Sidon sets over $\mathbb{F}_q \times \mathbb{F}_q$, accepted to Journal of Algebra Combinatorics Discrete Structures and Applications. Preprint.
  • Muhammad Afifurrahman. On the number of different entries in involutory MDS matrices over finite fields of characteristic two, AIP Conference Proceedings 2423, 060002 (2021). Preprint.

Other projects coming soon.
