Research

Broadly speaking, I am interested in various aspects of discrete mathematics, especially analytic number theory, combinatorics, and finite fields. I worked on some finite fields problems before coming for PhD. My PhD project concerns arithmetic statistics of algebraic groups. Problems arising from this project often lead to studying distributions of integer or rational solutions to some system of Diophantine equations. In particular, most problems in my thesis can be summarised as studying the growth of the number of such solutions as the ‘height’ of the solutions goes to infinity. In particular, works in my PhD thesis studies the arithmetic statistics of matrices groups and algebraic groups (arising from some multiplicative equations), through various techniques, mostly stemming from analytic number theory.
