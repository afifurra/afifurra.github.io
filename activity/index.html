Reading Groups

With Laurence Petrus Wijaya, I coordinate reading groups on mathematical papers in Bahasa Indonesia from 2023. The groups consisted of at most 5 students in each term, and each term explores different papers, as follows (chronologically):

  • D. Larsen, ‘Bertrand’s Postulate for Carmichael Numbers’, Int. Math. Res. Not., 15 (2023) 13072–13098. [2023]
  • M. Rudnev, S. Stevens, ‘An update on the sum-product problem’, Math. Proc. Camb., 173 (2023) 411-430. [2023]
  • D. Conlon, J. Fox, Y. Zhao, ‘A relative Szemerédi theorem’, GAFA 25 (2015), 733-762. [2023]
  • T. Tao, T. Ziegler, ‘Infinite partial sumsets in the primes’, J. Anal. Math. 151 (1), 375-389 [February-May 2024]
  • M. Bhargava, ‘Galois groups of random integer polynomials and van der Waerden’s Conjecture’, accepted to Ann. Math. [August-November 2024]
